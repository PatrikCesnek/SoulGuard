SGMT Trainings

I am very happy to announce, that for big demand SGMT trainings will again continue!

Trainings focused on molosoid breeds, AST, APBT and all active dogs with specific approach and motivation.

For everyne, who wishes to work with their dog propperly and also build better relationship in form of cooperation. Regardless whether you are an amateur, advanced,.. whether you have a puppy or senior with or without pp. We are here even for work-ambicious in sport kynology. Simply for everyone, who want to build relationship with their dog, socialize him or her and educate them selfs and eachother in friendly atmosphere of dogs and humans with profesionals.

Place: Areál cvičiska pri OMW Zelenec

Time: 10:30 hod.

On the place will be absolutely top figurant and cynologist Marek KUŠTÁR:

My first steps as a figurant have began after my return from military service in year 1999. I actively do this for 13 years. How many ?races?, bonitations and ?exams? I was figurant on is very difficul to write down, but for sure there were tens of them and hundreds of kilometers runned on trainings.

I will mention just those most important:

Since 2001 I am qualification figurant in all qualification ?races? and world championships of German Shephards WUSV in Slovakia.

In years 2004 - 2005 I have trained together with Austrian police. Austrian reprezentants are on top places with their Belgian Shephards Malinoison on FCI world championships.

In years 2001 to 2007 I was official figurant on qualification ?races? in Hungary by SCHH.

In December 2006 I trained in Spain in Valencia.

In June 2007 I was invited by spanish reprezentaion to two week training - preparation for world championship WUSV.

In year 2007 I was picked by german referees for being figurant on world championship of german shephards WUSV, whitch took place in October 2007 in Bratislava.

In year 2008 I qualified for FCI world championship and I participated in world championship of german shephards WUSV in USA as ?handler?.

In 2009 I was invited to do a seminar of ?defence? and obedience to Mexico, invitation from Mexican army.

In year 2011 seminar and visit in Police center in Mexico.

In year 2012 I participated in selection of figurants in RSV2000 in Germany. Dr. Helmut Reiser picked me as official figurant for ISP 2012.

May 2012 - training in Frankfurt am Main,

September 2012 - figurant on world championship of german shephards ISP (with international participation) in Korbach, Germany.

I am not only figurant, but also ?handler? and trainer of dogs. In 2008 I participated in qualification ?race? for world championship of german shephards. Thanks to hard work I have won this ?race? and I have become champion on Slovakia and national winner by SCHH. I have behind me great number of ?races? and ?exams?, not only with german shephards, but with different breeds as well.
